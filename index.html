Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our priority. Learn how we handle your data in Version 1.1.

Local Data Storage

Your workout logs and personal progress are stored locally on your device. We do not collect or share your individual training data with third parties.

HealthKit Integration

Titan accesses your step count data locally from Apple Health. This data is used only to visualize your daily activity within the app and is never transmitted off your device.

Anonymous Analytics

We use anonymized usage patterns (like popular training plans) to improve the app experience. No personally identifiable information or health data is ever collected.

iCloud Synchronization

If enabled, your data is securely synchronized across your Apple devices using Apple's end-to-end encryption through your personal iCloud storage.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by uninstalling the app. Since Titan does not use cloud accounts, your data is permanently removed upon uninstallation.
