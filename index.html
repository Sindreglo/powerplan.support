Privacy & Security

Your privacy is our priority. Learn how we protect your data.

Local data storage

All training data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or share your training data with third parties.

iCloud synchronization

If you have iCloud enabled, your data is securely synchronized across your Apple devices using end-to-end encryption.

Data security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All communication with our servers is encrypted with TLS.

No tracking

PowerPlan does not use tracking cookies or analytics tools that collect personal data. Your privacy is our priority.

Data deletion

You can delete all your training data at any time by uninstalling the app. Data is permanently removed from your device.
